The Daily Wonder

Volume 1 Edition Metropolis, Utah 10 July 2019

Man Up Look

Man looks up and sees the sky. It is blue. Wonderfully illuminated with clouds and dotted with sea gulls. He wonders how many others have done the same thing over the ages that men or women have looked up. Do you think you have ever done just like he has?
